How We Won 2nd Place at RidgeHacks with Graze

My friends and I participated in RidgeHacks 2024 today. We came in 2nd out of 22 teams (which were pretty much all high school teams) with our application named 'Graze'. We were able to beat both teams from BRHS, our district's high school, and potentially secured the best hackathon performance in our district's history (let me know if this is wrong because this is based on limited information). With this accomplishment, we were able to win hundreds of dollars in prizes ($300-$500) and will get featured in the BRHS C.O.D.E. club's classroom. You can check out our GitHub repository at https://github.com/pointySphere/graze and our Devpost submission at https://devpost.com/software/graze.
