My very first blog post!

Welcome to my first blog post! I am super thrilled and excited to share my journey throughout life through this blog! This is a space where I will share my thoughts, experiences, and teachings.

In this blog, I plan to write about a variety of topics that interest me. From math and programming, to travel and life, to personal development and sports - this blog will cover it all.

I am looking forward to this journey and I hope you will join me. - Alex Sheng
